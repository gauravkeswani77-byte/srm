Weighing the Flexibility Against the Demands

✅ Advantages

↑ Produces highly context-rich, reality-based theories
↑ Ideal for entirely new, disruptive, or under-researched topics
↑ Highly flexible — adapts as the data reveals new directions

⚠️ Limitations

! Notoriously time-consuming; requires managing massive amounts of unstructured data
! High risk of researcher bias influencing the coding process
! Findings lack broad statistical generalizability

Conclusion
